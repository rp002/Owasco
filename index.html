The Owasco Lake Cottage

A Memory Guide

Taking a walk down memory lane, our memories of "the cottage."

Dear Mom, Dad, Aunt Karen, Uncle Eddie, Uncle Fred, and Michael,

I have been thinking a lot lately about the cottage on Owasco Lake, the time you all spent there, and the memories you hold. I want to preserve those memories before they fade, and I need your help to do it.

I am working on a family memoir to honor the cottage and the time that was spent there. I am also considering writing a children's book — one I might call "Blackberry Gully" — inspired by my own memories of exploring the gully, walking on the moss-covered slippery rocks, picking blackberries.

Below you will find about 40 questions organized by theme. Please answer as many or as few as you would like. There is no pressure to participate at all. Some may spark a single sentence; others may open up a story you have not thought about in years. Every answer, however small, is a gift.

Just take a walk down memory lane and help me create something in honor of that special place in our hearts.

When you are ready, type your answers directly into the box under each question and hit the Submit button at the very end. The answers will be emailed directly to me.

With Love and Gratitude,
Michelle
